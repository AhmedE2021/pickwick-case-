Spicy Chai Mocktail

Ingredients:

  • 1 tea bag Pickwick Joy of Tea Spicy Chai
  • Glass of milk
  • 1 banana
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • ice cubes
  • mint leaf

Directions:

  • Brew a strong cup of Pickwick Joy or Tea Spicy Chai and let it cool.
  • When the tea has cooled, cut the banana into pieces and put it in the blender with the milk and cinnamon.
  • Fill the glass with ice cubes and then add the mocktail.
  • Garnish with some extra cinnamon and a mint leaf.*
  • * Consume immediately after cooling.

Green Tea Cheese cake

Ingredients:

  • 140 grams of biscuits of your choice ( Bastogne biscuits ,maria biscuits )
  • 80 grams of butter
  • 140ml hot water
  • 3 bags of Pickwick Green Tea
  • 140 ml cold water
  • 10 grams of gelatin
  • 500 ml whipped cream
  • 450 gr of cream cheese
  • 120 grams of sugar
  • Matcha powder

Directions:

  • Grind the cookies fine.
  • Melt the butter in a pan and stir in the crumbled biscuits. Place baking paper on the bottom of the cake pan (24 cm diameter). Press the butter-cake mixture on the bottom of the cake pan and put the bottom in the fridge for 20 minutes.
  • Soak the gelatin in the cold water.
  • Let the 3 green tea bags soak in the hot water.
  • Beat the whipped cream stiff.
  • Stir the cream cheese and sugar together.
  • Stir the green tea into the cream cheese mixture
  • Heat the gelatin in a little water and stir through the cream cheese mixture.
  • Stir the whipped cream into the mixture.
  • Spread the mixture on top of the cookie base, and put the cake tin in the fridge overnight.
  • Spread a little matcha powder over the top of the cake as a topping. Tasty!
