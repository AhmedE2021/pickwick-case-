Our Sustainability

Our goal at PICKWICK is to esure a better tea for our customers, good life for our farmers and to protect our planet.

How we do that?You can that below.

Ethical Tea Partnership

PICKWICK is one of the founding members of the Ethical Tea Partnership whick is committed to protect tea farmers and improve theeir living conditions. In Rwanda, for example, management training is given to tea farmers, they learn to invest smarter and more and more equality between men and women is being achieved.

Read more about Ethical Tea Partnership here

Rainforest Alliance

Fair cultivation is our top priority. That is why Pickwick obtains its tea leaves from Rainforest Alliance certified tea plantations. This way we know for sure that our tea is made sustainably and the farmer is supported. This year the Pickwick Herbal varieties are RA certified and our goal will be achieved in 2022; the entire Pickwick tea range is RA certified!

Read more about Rainforest Alliance here

Recycling

Our tea bags have been packaged in paper envelopes for many years. These envelopes are packed in cardboard boxes. This preserves the quality of the tea and allows you to distinguish the different tea flavors. That is so familiar. Remember that these paper packaging can be recycled. So if you don't use the packaging anymore, it can go straight to the old paper!
